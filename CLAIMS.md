# Claims and Boundaries

## Core principle

We measure temporal dependence, not will or intent.

CCI quantifies internal predictive consistency under input conditioning:
I(H_t ; H_{t+1} | E_t).

## Scope

- Diagnostic metric of internal temporal dependence.
- Not a causal proof.
- Not a direct measure of consciousness or metaphysical agency.

## Interpretation policy

- Metric layer (code, experiments, results): quantitative statements only.
- Interpretive layer (README/theory): visionary framing is allowed if explicitly
  labeled as interpretation/hypothesis.

## Threshold policy

Any mention of `1.0 bit` is operational and setup-specific, never universal.

Allowed phrasing:
- "In our setup, CCI > 1.0 coincides with strong internal temporal closure."

Disallowed phrasing:
- "CCI > 1.0 proves autonomous subjecthood."

## Risk disclosures

- Hidden variables: unmodeled inputs can inflate CCI.
- Trivial recurrence: deterministic loops can increase CCI without meaningful
  adaptive autonomy.
- Estimator assumptions: Gaussian approximation can miss nonlinear effects.
